Passion of design

Santakani was founded by three students from Aalto University. The same passion of design brought them together.

Inspired by creativity and diversity of individual designers and small design brands in Finland, the team sets its root in independent design. Rather than marketing and branding, independent design products reflect true values of design, the conbination of designers' skills and personalities.

In Santakani, every designer or brand has equal opportinuty to success. The design platform also protects original designs against plagiarism and price competition. The only simple rule: good design first.
